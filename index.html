What is teenQuiz?

teenQuiz is a three level brain challenge i.e: fresher, intermediate and master...
...read the question, answer it, get promoted to the next level and finally rule the challenge...
...You determine who wins which level.

    Keys
  • Masters set the challenges.
  • Challenges consist of a pair of 5 questions.
  • Complexity is based on the level of the challenge.

How to play teenQuiz??

in teenQuiz, you sign up then log in...
