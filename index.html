What is teenQuiz?

teenQuiz is a three level brain challenge which consists the fresher, intermediate and master levels, read the question, answer it, get promoted to the next level and finally rule the challenge. You determine who wins which level.

    Keys

  • Masters set the challenges.
  • Challenges consist of a pair of 5 questions.
  • Complexity is based on the level of the challenge.

How to play teenQuiz?

In teenQuiz, you sign up then log in and boom!, you have joined the challenge. Play and win the fresher class then go for the intermediate class.
In intermediate class, you participate in a more complex challenge compared to the fresher class, play win and go to the master class!
In the master class, you will not be challenged,you will challenge! Set challenges and rule the game!!!

Why teenQuiz?

teenQuiz is basically developed to improve teenage logic through the participation in the unguessable challenges.
Prove your logic and let others disprove it logically in teenQuiz!

teenQuiz does not need any installation. participate freely online works on any device as long as you are connected.
